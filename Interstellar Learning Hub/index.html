About Us

Founded by Mr. Cyborg Francis himself, he had a vision of a future where all life-forms could gather together and exchange knowledge that transcends our species' limitations. The Interstellar Learning Hub is a premier institution providing a world-class education to a diverse population of students from across the galaxy. Our mission is to foster academic excellence, innovation, and inter-species collaboration.
